ABOUT

Welcome to the Olde World Wands about page!

OWW is your go to for hand crafted woodworking.

We employ a variety of styles and all possess a colonial touch.

The OWW headmaster is an exuberant outdoors-man
and frequent participant in various re-enactments, which inspires his work.

The wand pictured was struck by lightning!

The shop was started in the beginning of 2015.

My dad has always been a great craftsman, and enjoys this hobby greatly.

In truth, he is a jack of all trades and a master of many!

Another favorite hobby of his is restoring antique tools.
